Spaghetti Bolognese

Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 1 hr | Serves: 4

Ingredients:
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g ground beef
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil
Instructions:
  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Saut√© onion & garlic in olive oil.
  3. Cook beef until browned.
  4. Add tomatoes & simmer 45 mins.
  5. Season & serve over spaghetti with basil.

Avocado Toast

Avocado Toast

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt, pepper, chili flakes (optional)
Instructions:

Toast bread. Mash avocado with seasoning. Spread on toast and serve.
