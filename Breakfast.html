Fluffy Pancakes

Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 15 mins | Serves: 2–4

Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
Instructions:
  1. In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Make a well in the center and add milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Mix until smooth, but do not overmix.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on top, then flip and cook 1–2 minutes more until golden.
  7. Serve warm with syrup, fruits, or powdered sugar.

Honey Cinnamon French Toast

Prep: 5 mins | Cook: 10 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients:
  • 4 slices bread
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Honey for drizzle
  • Fresh fruits (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Whisk eggs, milk, sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla in a bowl.
  2. Dip bread slices into mixture, soaking both sides.
  3. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Fry bread slices 2–3 minutes per side until golden brown.
  5. Serve with honey drizzle and optional fruits.
