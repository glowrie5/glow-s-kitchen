Spaghetti Bolognese

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 1 hr | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g ground beef
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until translucent.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in canned tomatoes, simmer for 45 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve sauce over spaghetti and garnish with basil.

Fluffy Pancakes

Perfect for breakfast or brunch, these pancakes are soft, fluffy, and super easy to make.

Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
Instructions:
  1. In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Mix until smooth. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on top and edges look dry, then flip.
  7. Cook for another 1–2 minutes until golden brown.

Tip: Serve with maple syrup, fresh berries, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar!

Avocado Toast

A healthy and quick breakfast or snack — simple, flavorful, and full of nutrients.

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices of whole-grain or sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 lemon (juiced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, chili flakes, poached egg, feta
Instructions:
  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
  2. Cut the avocado, remove the pit, and scoop into a bowl.
  3. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread avocado mixture evenly on the toasted bread.
  5. Add your favorite toppings — poached egg, sliced tomatoes, chili flakes, or feta cheese.
  6. Serve immediately for best taste and texture.

Tip: Drizzle with olive oil or balsamic glaze for extra flavor.
